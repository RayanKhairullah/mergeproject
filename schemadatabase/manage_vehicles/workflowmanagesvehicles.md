1. Vehicle workflow management:
1. User -> Vehicle Monitor (view mobile availability or simply view vehicle readiness and details of who is currently borrowing the vehicle)
2. User -> Rental form (can be accessed from the vehicle monitor or directly from the form) -> Fill in the data (select vehicle, borrower's name, PURPOSE & DESTINATION) -> Submit rental -> Redirected to the return form.
3. Return form -> fill in data (BORROWER'S NAME, FINAL KILOMETERS, FINAL SPEEDOMETER PHOTO)

2. WORKFLOW FOR OPERATIONAL VEHICLE READINESS:
admin/authorized personnel with access rights -> fill in the data (select vehicle, INSPECTION TIME (morning or afternoon), TIRE CONDITION (good or otherwise -> in the form of text notes), BODY AND FENDER CONDITION (good or other), WINDOW CONDITION (good or other), PHOTOS OF TIRE, BODY, AND FENDER CONDITION (IF ISSUES ARE FOUND - Maximum 3 photos), ODOMETER POSITION, SPEEDOMETER PHOTO, other notes)

Note: FINAL KILOMETER and KILOMETER POSITION must be synchronized! For example:
kilometer position: 13240 -> after rental: 13310 -> then the final kilometer position will automatically be 13310 when filling out vehicle readiness or the next rental. Likewise, after rental: 13310 -> filling out vehicle readiness: 13420 -> then the final kilometer will automatically be 13420 when performing the next rental or filling out vehicle readiness.

The point is that the kilometer data must be synchronized on the admin and user dashboards.

3. Various workflows:
FUEL/E-MONEY/PARKING/CAR WASH OPERATIONS, ETC.

User -> select the type of activity (Fuel, E-Money, Parking, Car Wash, Others)

Fuel type flow -> SELECT VEHICLE, NAME, FUND SOURCE (ADVANCE PAYMENT, PERSONAL FUNDS, SUKA_BAHARI CONSUMER COOPERATIVE), FUEL TYPE (PERTALITE, PERTAMAX, PERTADEX, PERTAMAX TURBO, Others (Retail or other), AMOUNT (Rp), LITERS, PHOTO DOCUMENTATION (Proof of Payment, Car (Rear), Fuel Gauge) required, Other Notes (optional).

E-Money/Parking/Car Wash/Other type flow -> SELECT VEHICLE, NAME, FUND SOURCE (ADVANCE PAYMENT, PERSONAL FUNDS, SUKA_BAHARI_CONSUMER_COOPERATIVE), PHOTO DOCUMENTATION (Proof of Payment (mandatory except for parking type), Activity Photo (mandatory)), Other Notes (optional).
